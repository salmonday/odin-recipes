Reuben Sandwich

These sandwiches are really delicious and easy to make.

A family fix-it-quick favorite. Serve with big bowls of steaming vegetable soup and dill pickles on the side. Enjoy!

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 8 slices rye bread
  • 1 slices deli sliced corned beef
  • 8 slices Swiss cheese
  • 1 cup sauerkraut, drained
  • 1/2 cup Thousand Island dressing

Steps

  1. Step 1: Preheat large skillet or griddle on medium heat.
  2. Step 2: Lightly butter one side of bread slices. Spread non-buttered sides with Thousand Island dressing. On 4 bread slices, layer 1 slice Swiss cheese, 2 slices corned beef, 1/4 cup sauerkraut and second slice of Swiss cheese. Top with remaining bread slices, buttered sides out.
  3. Step 3: Grill sandwiches until both sides are golden brown, about 5 minutes per side. Serve hot.
